## Sleep & Soothing

**⭐ Baby Nightgown / Knotted Gown**
Open or knotted bottom means diaper changes without fully waking the baby — no snaps, no zippers, no fumbling at 3am. A genuine game-changer.  I bought you a 3 pack of these, I hope you both enjoy them as much as I did!

**Hatch Rest Sound Machine / Night Light**
White noise around the clock, doubles as a night light, and you can control it from your phone without going into the room.  My wife bought one for our son and it's a nice sound machine and can help train your kids when to wake up.
- [hatch.co](https://www.hatch.co/rest)

**HALO SleepSack / Love to Dream Swaddle UP**
Makes swaddling easy and safe — way better than trying to wrap a burrito baby with a flat blanket at 3am. Temperature-regulating fabrics keep baby comfortable.
- [lovetodream.com](https://www.lovetodream.com/)
- [halosleep.com](https://www.halosleep.com/)

**⭐ Philips Hue Smart Bulb + Dimmer Remote**
Put a Hue bulb in the nursery lamp and keep the battery-powered dimmer remote on your nightstand. You can turn the light on dim before you even get out of bed, set the perfect low level for a feeding or diaper change, and turn it back off without getting up again. No fumbling for switches, no blasting bright light at 3am. Pairs with the Hue app for automations but works great with just the physical remote too.  Works great in most platforms as well.
- [Philips Hue Smart Bulbs](https://www.philips-hue.com/en-us/products/smart-light-bulbs)
- [Philips Hue Dimmer Remote — Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Philips-Hue-Installation-Free-Exclusively-562777/dp/B08W8GLPD5)

## Diapering

**⭐ Wipe Warmer**
Cold wipes on a baby's bottom = instant wake-up and crying. A warm wipe is one of those small things that keeps a sleepy baby calm during a diaper change. Look for one with a built-in night light so you're not turning on room lights mid-change.
- [Hiccapop Wipe Warmer — Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Warmer-Wipes-Dispenser-Holder-Changing/dp/B01LZJVSTE) — top-rated, top-heated design, night light with auto shutoff
- [Munchkin Warm Glow Wipe Warmer](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=munchkin+warm+glow+wipe+warmer) — budget-friendly, holds up to 100 wipes

> **A note on Diaper Genies:** Skip it. You end up with a smelly canister in the room that requires special bags, and in Daniel's experience just tossing diapers straight in the trash actually keeps things smelling better. Save the money.

## Lounging & Reclining

**Baby Bouncer / Rocker**
An essential for the first few months — a portable reclining seat you can carry from room to room so baby always has a safe, comfortable spot nearby while you cook, work, or just sit down for five minutes. Most move gently with baby's own weight and can be folded flat for storage. Aim for one with multiple recline positions and a machine-washable seat cover.
- [BabyBjörn Bouncer Balance Soft](https://www.babybjorn.com/products/baby-bouncers/bouncer-balance-soft/) — voted Best Overall 2025 by Parents Magazine, lightweight, no batteries needed, works from newborn to toddler
- [Ergobaby Evolve 3-in-1 Bouncer — Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=ergobaby+evolve+bouncer) — highly rated, great harness, adjustable height

**Infant Lounger (Dock-a-Tot / Snuggle Me style)**
These are the padded pillow-style loungers with raised sides and a recessed center divot that keeps baby nestled in place without rolling off surfaces. Great for supervised daytime lounging, tummy time, and giving baby a cozy spot that isn't the floor. **Important: for supervised use only — not for unsupervised sleep per safe sleep guidelines.**
- [Dock-a-Tot Deluxe+](https://www.amazon.com/dock-tot/s?k=dock+a+tot) — the most popular option, fits newborns through 8 months
- [Snuggle Me Organic](https://snugglemeorganic.com/) — organic cotton version, similar concept
